About Us

We Are Goal Shot BallAssociation of Bihar.

(Reg.No 200/2008)

Affiliated with the Goal Shot Ball Association of India and recognized by Asian and International Goal Shot Ball Federations. We promote this fast-paced hand-passing game among the young generation across Tamilnadu.

Where riders of all levels come together to learn, ride.

  • Affiliated with National & International Federations
  • Promoting Goal Shot Ball across Bihar.
  • Encouraging youth participation and sports spirit.
