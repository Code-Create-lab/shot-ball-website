About Us

We Are Goal Shot Ball Association of Bihar.

Reg. No 200/2008

Affiliated with the Goal Shot Ball Association of India and recognized by Asian and International Goal Shot Ball Federations. We promote this fast-paced hand-passing game among the young generation across Bihar.

Where athletes of all levels come together to learn, train and compete.

  • Affiliated with National & International Federations
  • Promoting Goal Shot Ball across Bihar
  • Encouraging youth participation and sports spirit
